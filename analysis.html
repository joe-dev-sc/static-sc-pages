Top 3 Strategic Improvements for Above-Market Performance

1. Operational Excellence & Margin Optimization

Target: Achieve 7-8% net profit margin by end of 2025

Actions:

  • Implement advanced cost management systems across all segments
  • Automate key production processes to reduce labor costs
  • Optimize biological asset management and feed conversion ratios
  • Establish segment-specific profit centers with clear accountability
  • Invest in predictive analytics for demand forecasting and inventory optimization
Expected Impact: +200-300 basis points margin improvement

2. Strategic Debt Restructuring & Capital Efficiency

Target: Reduce debt-to-equity ratio to 1.0-1.2 range

Actions:

  • Refinance high-cost debt with lower-rate alternatives
  • Optimize capital structure through strategic equity raises if needed
  • Implement cash flow forecasting and working capital optimization
  • Establish clear debt service coverage targets (>2.5x)
  • Create dedicated treasury function for sophisticated financial management
Expected Impact: ฿200-300M annual interest savings, improved financial flexibility

3. Digital Transformation & Market Leadership

Target: Become the most technologically advanced agri-food company in the region

Actions:

  • Implement IoT and AI solutions for biological asset monitoring
  • Develop direct-to-consumer e-commerce platforms
  • Create integrated supply chain management systems
  • Establish data analytics capabilities for market intelligence
  • Invest in sustainable farming technologies for ESG leadership
Expected Impact: 15-20% improvement in asset utilization, new revenue streams worth ฿2-3B
